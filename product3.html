Grand Opening Flowers

Celebrate new beginnings.

Grand opening flowers symbolize good wishes, prosperity and success for new businesses.

Image references: Root Flower Instagram

Additional flower etiquette tips

It is customary to include a congratualatory message card for grand openings. Avoid white flowers, as it is more common for sympathy arrangements.
